## 4. Low-Effort Social Activation (The "Lazy" System)

Since you are a solo dev, you cannot afford manual social media management. You must build an **Automated Distribution Pipeline**.

### The Pipeline Architecture

1.  **Source:** YouTube Long-form Video (The "Hero" Content).
2.  **Step 1 (Video):** Use **Opus Clip** or **Vizard.ai**.
    *   *Automation:* Automatically detects viral hooks, adds captions in your brand font, and re-frames for 9:16.
    *   *Output:* 3-5 Shorts/Reels/TikToks per main video.
3.  **Step 2 (Text):** Use **JohnGPT (Your RAG System)**.
    *   *Prompt:* "Read this transcript. Write a Twitter thread in my voice (Vibecoder persona) focusing on the technical challenge I solved."
    *   *Output:* 1 LinkedIn Article, 1 Twitter Thread.
4.  **Step 3 (Scheduling):** Use **Metricool** or **Buffer**.
    *   *Action:* Bulk upload the assets once a week. Let the scheduler handle the daily posting.

### The "Saxophone" Hack
*   **Instagram/TikTok:** Post raw, 15-second clips of you practicing sax with overlay text related to coding frustration (e.g., "Debugging Next.js errors requires jazz therapy"). This juxtaposed content often goes viral because it humanizes the "Tech Bro."
