How I Use AI to Deepen My Faith-Based Creativity

June 15, 2024 • 8 min read • By John Oluleke-Oke

As a filmmaker and developer who walks by faith, I've discovered that AI isn't just a tool for efficiency—it's a catalyst for deeper spiritual reflection in my creative process. Here's how I've integrated AI into my workflow while staying grounded in my Christian values...

My AI Faith Framework

I've developed a 3-part system for using AI ethically as a person of faith:

  1. Intentionality First - Before engaging any AI tool, I pray for clarity on the purpose
  2. Human Oversight - AI generates drafts, but I always add the "soul" through personal reflection
  3. Community Accountability - Sharing outputs with my small group before publishing
