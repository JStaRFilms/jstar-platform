Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary depending on scope, but most video projects take 2-4 weeks from concept to delivery, while web development projects typically take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you work with clients remotely?
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. Most of my process is designed for remote collaboration, including virtual meetings, cloud-based project management, and secure file sharing systems.
What's included in your video production service?
My video production services include pre-production planning, filming, post-production editing, color grading, sound design, and final delivery in multiple formats. I also provide revisions and source files upon project completion.
Can you integrate AI into existing systems?
Yes! I specialize in integrating AI capabilities into existing workflows and systems. This includes custom AI assistants, automation tools, and enhanced functionality for current applications.
