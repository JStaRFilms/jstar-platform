Gemini Pro

YouTube Script Framework

Here's a comprehensive framework for creating engaging YouTube video scripts:

1. Opening Hook (0:00-0:15)

Start with a bold statement, surprising fact, or provocative question that directly relates to your viewer's main pain point.

2. Problem Validation (0:15-0:45)

Acknowledge and empathize with your audience's struggles. Show you understand their specific challenges.

3. Solution Preview (0:45-1:15)

Tease the value you'll provide. Create anticipation for the transformation you'll offer.

4. Main Content (1:15-8:00)

Deliver on your promise with actionable insights, step-by-step instructions, or compelling storytelling.

5. Call-to-Action (8:00-8:30)

Guide viewers to the next step - subscribe, comment, watch another video, or visit a link.

Response Time: 2.4s
GPT-4o

YouTube Script Structure

Here's an effective framework for YouTube video scripts that drives engagement:

1. The Curiosity Gap (0:00-0:10)

Pose a question or present a scenario that creates immediate intrigue. Make viewers curious about the answer.

2. Relatability Bridge (0:10-0:30)

Connect with your audience by sharing a personal experience or common frustration they can relate to.

3. Value Proposition (0:30-1:00)

Clearly state what viewers will gain by watching the entire video. Set clear expectations.

4. Content Delivery (1:00-7:30)

Organize your main points with clear transitions. Use the "Rule of Three" for memorable takeaways.

5. Engagement Catalyst (7:30-8:00)

End with a thought-provoking question or challenge that encourages comments and interaction.

Response Time: 3.1s
Llama 3 70B

Video Script Template

Here's a versatile framework for creating compelling YouTube content:

1. The Promise Hook (0:00-0:12)

Make a specific, valuable promise about what viewers will learn or achieve by watching your video.

2. Credibility Establisher (0:12-0:30)

Briefly establish your authority or share a relevant success story to build trust immediately.

3. Problem Spotlight (0:30-1:00)

Deep-dive into the specific problem your audience faces, making it tangible and urgent.

4. Solution Journey (1:00-7:00)

Present your solution through examples, demonstrations, or step-by-step guidance with clear benefits.

5. Community Connector (7:00-7:30)

Invite viewers to join a community, share their experiences, or engage with related content.

Response Time: 2.8s
