YouTube Script Framework

1. Opening Hook (0-15s)

Start with a bold statement or a provocative question to grab attention.

2. Problem Validation (15-45s)

Empathize with your audience's struggles to show you understand them.

3. Main Content

Deliver on your promise with actionable insights and step-by-step instructions.

Analysis

Structured, empathetic approach. Best for tutorial content.

Response Time: 2.4s

YouTube Script Structure

1. The Curiosity Gap (0-10s)

Pose a question or present a scenario that creates immediate intrigue.

2. Relatability Bridge (10-30s)

Connect with your audience by sharing a personal, relatable experience.

3. Engagement Catalyst

End with a thought-provoking question that encourages interaction.

Analysis

Psychologically-driven. Excellent for vlogs and discussion videos.

Response Time: 3.1s

Video Script Template

1. The Promise Hook (0-12s)

Make a specific, valuable promise about what viewers will learn or achieve.

2. Credibility Establisher (12-30s)

Briefly establish your authority or share a success story to build trust.

3. Community Connector

Invite viewers to join a community or engage with related content.

Analysis

Value-focused and trust-building. Ideal for business/educational content.

Response Time: 2.8s
