Hi, I'm John Oluleke-Oke

Founder of J StaR Films. I help creators and businesses tell powerful stories through video, apps, and AI, all while exploring the deeper 'why' behind the work.

My Story

My journey into the world of creativity and technology started not with a grand plan, but with a simple curiosity. I picked up my first camera in college, not to become a filmmaker, but to document a mission trip. What I captured—the raw emotion, the untold stories—ignited something in me.

Fast forward a few years, and I found myself juggling a corporate tech job by day and editing client videos by night. I was building apps for efficiency while my soul craved the ineffable beauty of storytelling. It was a tension that felt unsustainable, until I realized it was my superpower.

J StaR Films was born from that intersection. It’s a space where I can leverage cutting-edge technology (be it AI tools or the latest app frameworks) not as an end, but as a means to amplify human stories and explore themes of faith, purpose, and growth.

I believe that creativity is not a luxury; it’s a fundamental part of being human. And technology, when used wisely, can be a powerful tool to democratize that creativity and connect us in profound ways.
