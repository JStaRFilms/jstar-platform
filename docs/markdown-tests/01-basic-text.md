## Special Characters & Escape Sequences

Asterisks: \*not italic\* and \*\*not bold\*\*

Underscores: \_not italic\_ and \_\_not bold\_\_

Backticks: \`not code\`

Hash: \# not a header

Brackets: \[not a link\](not-a-url)

Pipes: \| not table \|

Ampersand: Tom & Jerry, Apple & Orange

Less/Greater: 5 > 3 and 2 < 4
